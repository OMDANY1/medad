Medad Brand Identity Book 03.3 / Visual Direction / Photography Guidelines
01 / Minimal Environment
02 / Human Centered

Bright & Clean Environments

Photography should utilize soft, natural shadows, bright studio environments, and premium set materials. Never use dramatic low-key setups or dark backgrounds.

Accents of Green & Orange

Integrate subtle pops of #8BCF22 or #FF7A00 into props, background accents, or accessories to link the photographs to the visual identity.

Authentic Human Action

Depict actual Middle Eastern shoppers using technology. Avoid posed models. Capture realistic, pleasant, and candid digital shopping moments.
